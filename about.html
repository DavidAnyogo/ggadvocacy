Who We Are

Good Governance Advocacy International (GGAI) is a humanitarian organization focused on advocacy, training, and consulting. We are dedicated to promoting transparency, accountability, and governance that centers on people in Nigeria and beyond. Our goal is to strengthen institutions, empower communities, and build systems that support fairness and sustainable development.

Through our programs, we offer training and skill-building for leaders, policymakers, and citizens. We participate in policy discussions, engage with stakeholders, and provide consulting services to help organizations and government bodies adopt effective governance practices.

Meet The Visionary

Sir Leonard Anyogo is the founder and Director General of Good Governance Advocacy International (GGAI). He is an experienced legal and public affairs advocate, known for his strong belief in accountability, transparency, and justice in governance.

He earned his law degree from the Distinction of Masters in International Business Law University of Cumbria, United Kingdom. He was elected a member of the Chatham House in 2019, a seasoned author and policy expert. He has practiced law and used his voice through advocacy, media engagement, and public discussion to influence policy and raise awareness about citizens' rights. Under his leadership, GGAI has become a platform for training, civic education, and advocacy.
