Our Vision

Our Mission

To promote good governance, peace and progress, we provide training, and advocacy that empower people for sustainable development.

Core Values

To maintain integrity & transparency, we promote excellence and collaboration. This supports community growth and policy change.

What GGAI Does

Good Governance Advocacy International (GGAI) is committed to creating a world where leadership serves the people. We raise awareness through education and advocacy, offer training that prepares individuals and institutions and collaborate with governments and communities to promote accountability and transparency.
